Transforming an Invisible Blog into a Traffic Powerhouse

The Client: Travelling Weasels (Adapted)

The Challenge: A passionate travel blog with high-quality content was struggling to rank, receiving minimal organic traffic despite years of publishing.

My SEO Strategy:

  1. Technical & Content Clean-Up: Audited and fixed site speed, internal linking, and meta data.
  2. Strategic Content Expansion: Created cornerstone "pillar" content and supporting clusters.
  3. Authority Building: Executed a targeted digital PR campaign.
Organic Traffic: Increased by 285%, from 1,200 to 4,600+ monthly visitors within 6 months.
Keyword Rankings: Achieved top 3 rankings for 15 high-value travel keywords.
Authority: Secured featured spots in 3 major online travel publications.

Boosting Lead Generation for a B2B SaaS Platform

The Client: Testlify (Adapted)

The Challenge: A promising SaaS company in a competitive niche had a website that wasn't effectively generating marketing-qualified leads (MQLs) through organic search.

My SEO Strategy:

  1. Intent-Based Page Optimization: Restructured service pages to answer user queries.
  2. Content for the Buyer's Journey: Created bottom-of-funnel content like comparison guides.
  3. Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO): Redesigned and A/B tested CTAs and forms.
Keyword Rankings: Drove 5 key service pages to the first page of Google.
Lead Generation: Increased organic MQLs by 150% within one quarter.
Featured Snippets: Captured 3 featured snippets for "how to" questions.
